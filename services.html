Luthier & Repair Services

Levelling and Re-alignment

Precision adjustment of key heights and cup angles to ensure perfect pad seating, eliminating acoustic leaks and restoring effortless playability from top to bottom.

Instrument Detailing

A meticulous, deep-cleaning process that removes grime from complex mechanisms, restoring the instrument's mechanical fluidity and aesthetic gleam.

Re-cork & Re-pads

Careful removal and replacement of worn neck corks, tenon corks, and pads using premium materials to guarantee an airtight seal and a snappy, responsive feel.

Cleaning

A thorough bath using specialized solutions to safely strip away interior moisture buildup and oxidation, promoting optimal airflow and sanitary playing conditions.

Polish

Hand-buffing and specialized polishing treatments designed to safely lift tarnish and restore the original, brilliant luster of your instrument's finish.

Re-coating (Upon Request Only)

Custom spot-plating or full re-lacquering services to protect exposed brass and completely revitalize the instrument's exterior aesthetic.

Complete Overhaul

The ultimate restoration. Includes full disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, polishing, complete re-padding, re-corking, and precise acoustic balancing to make vintage or heavily used horns play better than new.

Reposting

Expert structural repair and silver-soldering of broken or detached key posts to ensure exact mechanical alignment and long-term stability.

Complete Resoldering and Overhaul

Major structural rehabilitation for severely damaged instruments, combining our complete overhaul process with extensive dent removal and foundational joint resoldering.

Spring Calibration

Precision tensioning and replacement of blued steel or needle springs to customize key action, ensuring a light, lightning-fast, and even touch across the entire horn.
